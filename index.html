About Us

The University of British Columbia Human Powered Submarine Team (SUBC) is an engineering student team that designs, builds, and races human powered submarines. Founded in 1989 by a small group of mechanical engineering students, SUBC is UBCâ€™s longest lived engineering design team. The team was created to participate in the second International Submarine Races (ISR2), and has since had many successes with both one and two-person submarines. Most recently, the team placed in the top 5 for our design at our first-ever European competition (eISR3).
